# MI Global Study — Premium Website Plan

## 1) Project Goal
Build a simple yet premium website for **Global Study Limited (MI Global Study)** that clearly communicates trust, service depth, and destination expertise, then converts visitors into consultation bookings.

---

## 2) Brand Positioning
**Core promise:** One trusted partner from career planning to campus arrival.

**Trust signals to emphasize:**
- 200+ university partners worldwide
- End-to-end support model
- Scholarship and visa expertise
- Strong outcomes across traditional and emerging destinations

---

## 3) Audience Segments
- Students seeking bachelor’s, master’s, and professional pathways abroad
- Parents/families focused on safety, affordability, and outcomes
- Scholarship-focused applicants
- Students targeting specialized country pathways (e.g., China MBBS, South Korea tech/research)

---

## 4) Information Architecture (Simple + Premium)

### Core Pages
1. **Home**
2. **Services**
3. **Study Destinations**
4. **Scholarships**
5. **Success Stories**
6. **About Us**
7. **Contact / Book Consultation**

### Destination Subpages
- UK, USA, Canada, Australia
- Germany, Netherlands, Sweden, Denmark, Poland
- South Korea
- China (MBBS, Engineering, Economics)
- Malaysia, Thailand

---

## 5) Homepage Structure
1. **Hero section**
   - Headline: “Your Global Education Journey, End-to-End.”
   - Primary CTA: **Book Free Counseling**
   - Secondary CTA: **Explore Destinations**

2. **Credibility strip**
   - “Representing 200+ prestigious universities worldwide.”

3. **6-Step Service Journey**
   - Counseling → University Selection → Applications → Scholarships → Visa → Pre/Post-Arrival

4. **Destination Highlights**
   - Traditional destinations + emerging regions

5. **Scholarship Opportunity Block**
   - Focus on affordability and guidance process

6. **Success Stories**
   - Student outcomes, testimonials, placements

7. **Final CTA + Quick Form**
   - Intake-focused consultation form and WhatsApp button

---

## 6) Content Strategy by Destination

### Europe
- Position Germany as tuition-efficient / low-cost option
- Feature Netherlands, Sweden, Denmark, Poland as quality alternatives
- Explain academic quality, affordability, and visa pathways

### South Korea
- Highlight technology/research strengths
- Scholarship pathways and admission support

### China
- Strong pathways for MBBS, Engineering, Economics
- Scholarship emphasis (e.g., CSC and institutional funding)

### Southeast Asia
- Promote Malaysia and Thailand for budget-friendly, quality education

### Traditional Destinations
- UK, USA, Canada, Australia: brand familiarity + broad course options + clear process timelines

---

## 7) UX + UI Direction (Premium but Simple)
- Minimal layout with generous white space
- High-contrast typography and clear hierarchy
- Deep navy + gold accent palette
- Clean iconography and card-based content blocks
- Light motion effects (subtle, performance-friendly)
- Mobile-first responsive behavior

---

## 8) Conversion Features (High Priority)
- Sticky “Book Consultation” CTA
- WhatsApp quick-connect button
- Short lead form on every key page
- Country/program selector in forms
- Downloadable checklist lead magnet
- Intake urgency bar (Fall/Spring applications)

---

## 9) SEO Foundation
- One optimized page per destination
- Program-specific intent pages (e.g., MBBS in China)
- FAQ schema + Local Business schema
- Internal linking from blogs to destination pages
- Content plan:
  - Scholarship updates
  - Visa process guides
  - Country comparisons
  - Budget planning articles

---

## 10) Suggested Tech Setup
- **Frontend:** Next.js (or Webflow for faster launch)
- **CMS:** Sanity/Contentful/Webflow CMS
- **Forms + CRM:** HubSpot or Zoho integration
- **Analytics:** GA4 + Meta Pixel
- **Performance:** Image optimization, CDN, caching, and Core Web Vitals optimization

---

## 11) Delivery Roadmap (4 Weeks)
- **Week 1:** Discovery, sitemap, low-fidelity wireframes, content outline
- **Week 2:** Visual design system, high-fidelity homepage + page templates
- **Week 3:** Development, CMS setup, integrations, content entry
- **Week 4:** QA, SEO baseline, launch readiness, go-live

---

## 12) MVP Scope (Fast Launch)
- Home
- Services
- Study Destinations (+ 4 key subpages: Germany, South Korea, China, Malaysia/Thailand)
- Scholarships
- Contact / Book Consultation

---

## 13) Success Metrics
- Consultation booking conversion rate
- Form completion rate
- WhatsApp click-through rate
- Destination-page engagement
- Organic traffic growth on destination and scholarship keywords
